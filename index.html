Coding Quiz

Embark on an amazing yet dangerous multiple-choice question adventure in the magical world of 'Coding.'


You'll test your metal by learning the archaic arts of 'Computer Code' through a series of difficult tasks & questions. At the end of your adventure, your high-score will be logged in the books of eternity.


Each round of questions & tasks adds each players' score to the global-scoreboard: this will show who's the 'best-of-the-best' and the reigning Coding Wizard Champion!!!

Instructions:

  • You've got 60 seconds (timer seen at the top-right of the screen) to complete all, or as many as you can, of the questions.
  • Each question provides four (4) different options to choose from.
  • If you choose correctly, you will get one (1) point.
  • If you choose wrong, you will get zero (0) points,
    AND suffer 'timer-burn' (aka, time-stolen and deducted from the ticking-down clock).
  • At the end of the Quiz, your total score will be displayed. Maximum score is 24 points.
  • On the 'Quiz Result' page, type your initials and click 'Add' to save and log your score on the leader-board, .
  • Otherwise, you may leave the input section 'Blank', and no data will be saved to the leader-board on the High-Scores page.


Click 'Start Quiz'to find out if you're worthy enough to be crowned the next Coding Wizard Champion!!!!!
